## 🎯 **Design Philosophy**

### **Core Principles**

1. **🧠 Intelligence-First Design**
   - AI agents as first-class citizens in the architecture
   - Human-AI collaboration at every interaction point
   - Continuous learning and adaptation

2. **🔄 Adaptive Personalization**
   - Every explanation tailored to user skill level
   - Cultural and domain-specific adaptations
